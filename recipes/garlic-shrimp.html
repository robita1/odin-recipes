Garlic-shrimp

Garlic Shrimp Ingredients

These spicy garlic shrimp are my favorite way to dazzle in a hurry. Full of flavor and takes minutes to make! Serve immediately over pasta, with warm crusty bread and a garden salad, or with a rice dish - they take center stage at any meal.

Ingredients

  • ⅓ cup extra-virgin olive oil
  • 3 cloves garlic, sliced
  • 1 teaspoon red pepper flakes
  • 2 teaspoons paprika
  • 2 pounds shell-on deveined jumbo shrimp
  • ¼ cup lemon juice
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh basil
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • ¼ teaspoon black pepper

Steps

  1. Start by heating oil in a large skillet.
  2. Add garlic and cook until it becomes fragrant, fir about 1 minute.
  3. Add red pepper flakes and paprika to the oil, followed by shrimp.
  4. Pour in lemon juice and allow to cook until the shrimp are bright pink on the outside and the meat is no longer transparent.
  5. After 1 to 2 minutes reduce heat to medium low.
  6. Add basil and toss lightly.
  7. finally, Season with salt and pepper to serve.
